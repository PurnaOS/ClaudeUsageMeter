# NFR-0003 — Recognizable brand mark and app icon

The app shall carry a consistent brand mark — a gauge/speedometer figure — shown
in the menu bar and the detail-window header, and shall ship a macOS app icon
(`.icns`) rendered from the same mark, so the app is identifiable at a glance and
in Login Items / Finder.
